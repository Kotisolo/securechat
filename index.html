Military-grade security

The server knows nothing.

We built SecureChat so that even if our servers were seized, there would be nothing to find.

🔑
ECDH P-256 Key Exchange
Each user generates a keypair in their browser. Public keys are exchanged. The shared secret is derived locally via elliptic-curve Diffie-Hellman and never transmitted.
X25519 / P-256
🔄
Double Ratchet Algorithm
Inspired by the Signal Protocol. Every message uses a unique derived key. Compromise of one session key cannot expose past or future messages.
FORWARD SECRECY
🛡️
AES-256-GCM Encryption
Each message encrypted with a 256-bit key and a cryptographically random 96-bit IV. GCM mode provides authentication — any tampering is detectable.
256-BIT KEY
🚫
Zero Knowledge Architecture
Our server stores only the IV and ciphertext — never the keys, never the plaintext. Even we cannot read your messages. Zero data to hand over.
ZERO KNOWLEDGE
📡
Encrypted in Transit
All traffic runs over TLS. Voice and video calls use WebRTC with DTLS-SRTP — the same standard used by secure telephony.
TLS + DTLS-SRTP
✅
Safety Number Verification
Every conversation has a unique safety number. Compare it with your contact in person to verify no one is intercepting your messages.
VERIFY IDENTITY

Simple to use

Up and running
in 60 seconds.

1
Create your account
Register with your name, phone number and a password. Your encryption keys are generated instantly in your browser — we never see them.
2
Keys are generated on your device
The moment you register, your browser generates an ECDH P-256 keypair. Your private key never leaves your device. Ever.
3
Find your contacts
Anyone who has registered appears in your contact list. Tap their name to open a conversation — the shared secret is computed automatically.
4
Start chatting — fully encrypted
Every message you send is encrypted with AES-256-GCM before it leaves your device. The server relays encrypted bytes it cannot decode.
